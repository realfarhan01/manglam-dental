What Are Veneers?

Veneers are ultra-thin shells of ceramic or composite resin that are bonded to the front surface of your teeth to enhance their shape, color, and alignment. They are one of the most effective cosmetic treatments available for creating a flawless, confident smile.

What Issues Can Veneers Fix?

Veneers are perfect for patients who want to correct:

  • Discolored or stained teeth
  • Worn down or chipped teeth
  • Uneven, misaligned, or irregularly shaped teeth
  • Gaps between teeth

The Smile Makeover Process with Veneers

Our cosmetic dentists follow a precise and comfortable treatment plan:

  • Tooth preparation and impression taking
  • Temporary veneers (if needed)
  • Bonding of your custom-designed veneers
  • Final polish and reveal of your new smile!
