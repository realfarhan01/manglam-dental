What Is Teeth Whitening?

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that removes stains and discoloration from the enamel, giving your teeth a noticeably whiter appearance. It can be done professionally at a dental clinic or using at-home kits, though the in-office procedure guarantees faster and more reliable results.

At Manglam Dental Hospital, we offer safe, effective, and long-lasting whitening treatments, tailored to your specific dental needs. Our signature service, Bright Smile Whitening in Jaipur, is designed to rejuvenate your smile in just one visit.

Causes of Teeth Discoloration

Before diving into whitening treatments, it’s important to understand what causes teeth to lose their natural whiteness:

  • Food and Beverages: Coffee, tea, red wine, and cola are major culprits.
  • Smoking or Tobacco Use: Nicotine and tar can deeply stain teeth.
  • Poor Dental Hygiene: Inadequate brushing or flossing leads to plaque buildup.
  • Aging: As enamel wears down over time, the yellowish dentin beneath becomes visible.
  • Medications: Certain antibiotics or antihistamines may discolor teeth.
  • Fluorosis: Excess fluoride during childhood can cause enamel discoloration.

Understanding these factors can help in choosing the most effective whitening solution and maintaining the results afterward.

Teeth Whitening Procedure at Manglam Dental Hospital

Wondering what to expect during your whitening session? Here’s a step-by-step breakdown:

Initial Examination:

We begin with a comprehensive check-up to assess your oral health and determine your suitability for whitening treatment.

Teeth Cleaning:

Plaque and tartar are removed through professional cleaning, ensuring better whitening results.

Application of Whitening Gel:

A peroxide-based whitening gel is applied to the teeth. In Laser Teeth Whitening Jaipur, this gel is then activated using a laser device.

Laser Activation

The laser enhances the effect of the whitening gel, allowing it to penetrate the enamel and remove deep stains.

Rinse and Reveal:

After 30–45 minutes, the gel is removed, and the transformation is instantly visible.

Post-Treatment Advice:

We provide guidance on maintaining your bright smile and avoiding foods or habits that can re-stain teeth.
