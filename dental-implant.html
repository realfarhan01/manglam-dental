What is a Dental Implant?

A dental implant is a titanium screw surgically placed into the jawbone to act as an artificial tooth root. Once it integrates with the bone (Osseointegration), it provides a strong and stable foundation for placing a crown, bridge, or denture.

Dental implants are considered the gold standard in Permanent Tooth Replacement in Jaipur, offering unmatched durability, comfort, and aesthetics.

Why Choose Dental Implants?

Dental implants are a long-term solution for:

  • Missing one or more teeth
  • Supporting dentures for added stability
  • Enhancing chewing and speaking ability
  • Preventing bone loss due to missing teeth
  • Improving facial aesthetics and symmetry

Compared to bridges or removable dentures, implants offer a more natural and permanent solution. At Manglam Dental Clinic, our experts ensure each implant is customized for perfect alignment, function, and comfort.
