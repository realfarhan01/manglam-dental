What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a dental procedure used to treat infected or damaged tooth pulp. When the pulp (the soft tissue inside your tooth containing nerves and blood vessels) becomes inflamed or infected due to deep decay, cracks, or trauma, a root canal is necessary to save the tooth and eliminate pain.

During the procedure, the infected pulp is removed, the canals are cleaned and disinfected, and the space is filled and sealed. A crown is often placed afterward to restore the tooth’s strength and function.

At Manglam Dental Hospital, we specialize in Painless Root Canal in Jaipur, ensuring your comfort at every step.

Signs You May Need a Root Canal

  • Persistent toothache or pain while chewing
  • Sensitivity to hot or cold that lingers
  • Swollen or tender gums
  • Darkening or discoloration of the tooth
  • Pimple-like swelling near the affected tooth
