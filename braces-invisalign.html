Braces / Invisalign – The Path to a Perfect Smile at Manglam Dental Hospital, Jaipur

Orthodontic treatment has come a long way from bulky metal braces and uncomfortable adjustments. Today, individuals looking to straighten their teeth can choose from a variety of advanced and discreet options—including Invisalign clear aligners and modern braces—all available at Manglam Dental Hospital, Jaipur.

Whether you're a teenager facing your first orthodontic journey or an adult seeking a smile upgrade, Manglam Dental Hospital provides personalized care and cutting-edge solutions to help you achieve a healthy, confident smile.

Why Orthodontic Treatment Matters

Crooked, crowded, or misaligned teeth aren't just cosmetic concerns—they can affect your bite, cause jaw pain, lead to premature tooth wear, and even contribute to gum disease. Orthodontic treatments like braces or Invisalign correct these issues, improving both the appearance and function of your teeth.

Braces vs. Invisalign – What's Right for You?

Traditional Braces

Traditional metal braces remain one of the most effective methods for correcting severe misalignments. These braces consist of brackets attached to each tooth, connected by wires that gradually move your teeth into the desired position.

  • Ideal for: complex cases, children, teens
  • Cost: ₹25,000 – ₹70,000
  • Maintenance: Regular in-clinic adjustments
Ceramic Braces

Ceramic braces are similar to metal ones but use tooth-colored or clear brackets for a less noticeable look.

  • Ideal for: teens and adults wanting subtle treatment
  • Cost: ₹40,000 – ₹90,000
Lingual Braces

These braces are placed behind the teeth, making them completely invisible from the front.

  • Ideal for: professionals or public-facing individuals
  • Cost: ₹80,000 – ₹1,50,000
Invisalign (Clear Aligners)

Invisalign offers a virtually invisible and removable solution using clear plastic trays customized to your teeth. At Manglam Dental Hospital, we use digital scanning to create a 3D model of your smile, allowing us to plan your treatment in detail.

  • Ideal for: adults and teens with mild to moderate alignment issues
  • Cost: ₹70,000 – ₹2,50,000
  • Advantages: removable, comfortable, aesthetic

How the Treatment Works

Initial Consultation – We evaluate your teeth, bite, and facial structure.

Digital Scans – A 3D image of your mouth is created for precise planning.

Custom Plan – Based on your needs, we recommend the right solution: braces or Invisalign.

Active Treatment Phase – Your teeth gradually move into place with regular monitoring.

Retention – After treatment, we provide retainers to maintain your new smile.
