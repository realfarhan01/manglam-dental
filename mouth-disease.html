What is Mouth Disease? Common Oral Health Issues Explained

Mouth diseases, also known as oral diseases, encompass a wide range of conditions that affect your gums, tongue, lips, and inner cheeks. These can range from common issues like gum disease and tooth decay to serious infections such as oral thrush or even precancerous lesions.

Why Do Mouth Diseases Occur?

Mouth diseases can be caused by:
  • Poor oral hygiene
  • Smoking or tobacco use
  • Uncontrolled diabetes
  • Viral or bacterial infections
  • Weak immune system
  • Genetic predisposition

If left untreated, even minor issues can escalate into severe infections. That’s why early detection and professional care are crucial.
