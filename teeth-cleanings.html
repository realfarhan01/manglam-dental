Why Professional Teeth Cleaning Is Important

Professional teeth cleaning is more than just a cosmetic service. It plays a vital role in preventing oral health issues. Regular brushing and flossing can only clean about 60-70% of the tooth surface. The remaining 30%—especially below the gumline and between teeth—can become a breeding ground for bacteria.

At Manglam Dental Hospital, we emphasize the significance of professional cleaning as part of our Oral Hygiene Services in Jaipur. Our trained dentists and hygienists use advanced tools and techniques to ensure your teeth are not only clean but also protected against long-term damage.

What is Dental Scaling and Polishing?

Dental Scaling and Polishing is a two-step process designed to remove plaque, tartar, and stains from the teeth:

Scaling:

This involves the removal of hardened plaque (calculus) and tartar from both above and below the gumline. Special ultrasonic devices are used for effective and pain-free cleaning. Scaling helps prevent gum disease and tooth decay, especially in areas difficult to clean at home.

Polishing:

Once scaling is complete, polishing smooths the surface of your teeth, making it harder for plaque to accumulate. Polishing also removes surface stains, giving your teeth a brighter, whiter appearance.

At Manglam Dental Hospital, we provide gentle and effective Dental Scaling and Polishing in Jaipur, ensuring both your comfort and your oral health.

Benefits of Professional Teeth Cleaning

Opting for professional cleaning at regular intervals offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Prevention of cavities and gum disease
  • Elimination of bad breath (halitosis)
  • Whiter, brighter smile
  • Early detection of dental problems
  • Improved overall health, including a lower risk of heart disease and diabetes

How Often Should You Get Teeth Cleaning?

Most dental professionals recommend getting a professional cleaning every six months. However, patients with gum issues or a history of plaque buildup may need more frequent visits.
